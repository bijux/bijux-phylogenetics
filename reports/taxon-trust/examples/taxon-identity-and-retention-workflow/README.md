# Taxon Identity And Retention Workflow

This example binds one reviewer-facing evidence bundle to the roadmap goals that
establish taxon trust from raw labels through workflow retention:

- goal `21`: spelling and formatting drift
- goals `22` to `24`: synonym detection and controlled resolution
- goals `25` to `26`: namespace classification and mixed-namespace detection
- goals `27` to `30`: crosswalk, exclusion reasoning, workflow loss, and
  stability
